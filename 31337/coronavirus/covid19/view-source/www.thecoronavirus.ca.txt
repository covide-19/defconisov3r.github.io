<h1>What is the Corona Virus?</h1>
<p>
Coronavirus is a family of viruses and "The Corona Virus" from Wuhan China is being called
<a target="_blank" href= "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Novel_coronavirus_(2019-nCoV)">'2019-nCoV'</a> by scientists.
It is a contagious virus that causes respiratory infection and has shown evidence of human-to-human transmission.
</p><p>The 2019-nCov coronavirus is also being referred to informally as the "Wuhan coronavirus" or "novel coronavirus".
Although unproven, the Wuhan coronavirus is assumed to have jumped from bats to humans through contact at the Huanan market in Wuhan.
Genetic sequencing has shown similarities to SARS-CoV and SARS-like bat coronavirus supporting this theory.
</p>
<p>
The <a target="_blank" href= "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Novel_coronavirus_(2019-nCoV)">'2019-nCoV'</a>
coronavirus outbreak began in Wuhan, Hubei, China, is spreading around Asia, and has arrived in Canada and the United States.
The <a target="_blank" href="https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019">World Health Organization</a> has declared a world health emergency as of Jan 30th 2020.

</p>

<h2>Will the Corona Virus become a Pandemic?</h2>
<p>
A <a target="_blank" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pandemic">pandemic</a> is the rapid spread of a disease all over the world. As of Jan 30th 2020
the World Health Organization has declared a world health emergency. While the quantity and severity of cases is difficult to predict it seems likely that the corona virus
will become a pandemic. </p>
<p>
<a target="_blank" href="https://www.news-medical.net/news/20200124/Coronavirus-could-turn-into-a-new-pandemic.aspx">
According to Jeff Hogan</a>, a professor and infectious disease expert at the University of Georgia, who studied the SARS coronavirus extensively the Wuhan coronavirus
will likely become a pandemic. In the interview he is recorded as saying, "While WHO and other authorities have not declared this a pandemic,
I do expect this type of announcement unless the rate of new cases begins to decline rapidly in the next 7-10 days."
</p>
<p>Canadian infectious disease specialist <a target="_blank" href="https://torontosun.com/news/local-news/warmington-infectious-disease-specialist-says-were-prepared-for-coronavirus">
Dr. Michael Gardam</a> encourages everyone to be calm saying, “My life is way more dangerous walking in downtown Toronto and getting run over by somebody than worrying about
this coronavirus killing me.”</p>

<h2>How to prepare for a Corona Virus Pandemic.</h2>
<p>The SARS outbreak in 2003 was controlled by public health measures such as quarantine, hand washing, surveillance of travelers and the use of advanced respiratory protection for healthcare workers. These measures are almost certain to be just as effective during the current outbreak."</p>
<p>The best preparations for a coronavirus pandemic are preventative. A well written opinion piece on the <a href="https://nationalpost.com/opinion/np-view-the-wuhan-virus-may-not-be-it-but-the-next-big-pandemic-is-coming-are-we-ready" target="_blank"> National Post</a> draws attention to the fact that the Canadian medical system is already overburdened without a pandemic, so let's just try to stay healthy.</p>
<p>The corona virus is thought to spread mainly through respiratory droplets from coughs and sneezes.
Avoid contact with infected people and clean surfaces and hands so that virus particles can't get into your body.
The most useful supplies such as <a href="https://amzn.to/2vwz3NN" target="_blank">hand-sanitizers</a>,
<a href="https://amzn.to/36LvrEs" target="_blank">cleaning supplies</a> and <a href="https://amzn.to/2S50ING" target="_blank">face masks</a>
are easy to find right now, but should demand increase or shipments get delayed they may be in short supply.</p>

<p><a href="https://www.news-medical.net/news/20200131/No-Masking-the-Best-Way-To-Avoid-The-Scary-Coronavirus-Wash-Your-Hands.aspx" target="_blank">Don't depend on face masks</a>
to protect you, masks can get contaminated as well, and infective droplets can enter your body through contact with contaminated surfaces. Avoiding sick people
and practicing careful handwashing and cleaning practices is much more effective than wearing a mask. If you are purchasing masks to prepare for the coronavirus,
please also print the <a href="https://www.who.int/publications-detail/advice-on-the-use-of-masks-the-community-during-home-care-and-in-health-care-settings-in-the-context-of-the-novel-coronavirus-(2019-ncov)-outbreak" target="_blank">document by the WHO</a> on how to properly use masks in the context of the coronavirus.</p>

<p>If you expect to self-quarantine or avoid grocery stores <a href="https://amzn.to/2OgdVSA" target="_blank">extra food supplies</a> would also be good. The current quarantine period is 14 days, so have 14 days food and water.
Remember, preparation is only preperation if it's done ahead of a crisis, don't wait until the last minute.</p>

</p>
