<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-y7PqH3zGOFQ/Xm8yraZVsGI/AAAAAAAAAAU/qvbc4UTIYQwepvzbLV4buvlyO4qw8oHhQCLcBGAsYHQ/s1600/cov.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="color: #4d469c; margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em; text-decoration-line: none;"><img border="0" data-original-height="321" data-original-width="506" height="203" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-y7PqH3zGOFQ/Xm8yraZVsGI/AAAAAAAAAAU/qvbc4UTIYQwepvzbLV4buvlyO4qw8oHhQCLcBGAsYHQ/s320/cov.jpg" style="background: transparent; border-radius: 0px; border: 1px solid transparent; box-shadow: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.2) 0px 0px 0px; padding: 8px; position: relative;" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><span style="color: red;"><b>Stay away from crowd if you feel infected</b></span>&nbsp;</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><span style="color: blue;"><b>Take precaution while going public</b></span></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><span style="color: blue;"><b><br /></b></span></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="background-color: white; clear: both; color: #444444; font-family: Arial, Tahoma, Helvetica, FreeSans, sans-serif; font-size: 13px; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="sf-content-block content-block" style="background-color: white; box-sizing: border-box; color: #444444; float: left; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; width: 900.75px;">
<div style="box-sizing: border-box;">
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: 20px; margin: 0px; position: relative; text-align: justify;">
Wash your hands frequently with soap and water or alcohol thoroughly&nbsp;.</h3>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: 20px; margin: 0px; position: relative; text-align: justify;">
Maintain social distancing<span style="font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;at least 1 metre (3 feet) distance between yourself and anyone who is coughing or sneezing.</span></h3>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: 20px; margin: 0px; position: relative; text-align: justify;">
Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth.</h3>
<h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 18px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: 20px; margin: 0px; position: relative; text-align: justify;">
Practice respiratory hygiene.</h3>
<div>
<b>If you have any&nbsp;<span style="font-family: &quot;arial&quot; , &quot;helvetica&quot; , sans-serif; font-size: 18px;">fever, cough and difficulty breathing</span>&nbsp;, immediately seek medical care</b></div>
