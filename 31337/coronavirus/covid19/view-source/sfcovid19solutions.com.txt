                   <h3>Dear City and County Officials and Elected Leaders,<br></h3>



<p></p>



<p></p>



<p>In our communities, what hurts some of us, hurts all of us. Our response to this crisis must prioritize the collective needs of all people.&nbsp;<br></p>



<p><strong>Join us in calling on our government officials and elected leaders to immediately address the platform below. To survive, we must be a city #UnitedInCrisis.</strong><br></p>



<p>As San Francisco community organizations and labor unions serving those most impacted by COVID-19, we have highlighted a 10 point platform for the city to address immediately. While some corporations and individuals may be trying to keep the status quo, the first priority of our government must be to the people in our communities.<br></p>



<p><strong>Sign below </strong>to call on all decision makers and community members to immediately act in response to this crisis by supporting our most vulnerable San Franciscans.</p>



<p></p>



<p></p>



<p></p>



<p><strong>Signed,<br>SF United in Crisis, <a href="/coalition-members/">click to see all member organizations here.&nbsp;</a></strong></p>
