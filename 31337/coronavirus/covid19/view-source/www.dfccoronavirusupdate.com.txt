<li>Wash your hands frequently with soap and warm water for 20 seconds or using an alcohol-based sanitizer.</li>
<li>Cover coughs and sneezes with tissues or in the sleeve of your own shirt.</li>
<li>Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth (especially with unwashed hands).</li>

<li>Stay home if you are feeling unwell.</li>
<li>If you are caring for an ill person, stay home for a two-week period (estimated incubation time).</li>
