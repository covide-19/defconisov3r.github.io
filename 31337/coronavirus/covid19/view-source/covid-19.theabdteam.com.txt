				<div class="elementor-widget-container">
					<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">To our valued clients, colleagues, and community,
<br>
<br>
Since our founding, ABD’s top priority has been to support our clients, colleagues and communities. We understand that many of our clients and members of our communities are experiencing hardship caused by COVID-19. Our thoughts and prayers go out to all as we navigate these rough waters. Together we will flatten the curve of COVID-19.
<br>
<br>
We wanted to take a moment to share the measures we are taking to ensure uninterrupted service to you and to bring your attention to the ABD COVID-19 Communications portal.
<br>
<br>
<strong>100% Open for Business</strong>
<br>
<br>
Our culture and focus on operational expertise have allowed us to create a team that lives our values of being accountable, committed, forward thinking, expert and in this together. These values inform our operating principles, which enable our team to be 100% operational in support of our clients.
<br>
<br>
<strong>Our Teams and Our Offices</strong>
<br>
<br>
We went fully virtual last Wednesday, March 11<sup>th</sup>. All of our employees have the resources required to work remotely at full capacity, enabling them to collaborate, facilitate meetings, and serve client requests. Offices will remain closed through April 7th, and then the decision will be revisited.
<br>
<br>
<strong>ABD Connect</strong>
<br>
<br>
Please take advantage of the <a href="https://clientportal.vertafore.com/Login/1006527-1">ABDConnect</a> online platform. It provides 24/7 access to manage your information including policies, certificate issuance, and claims, at any time and from anywhere. If you require access, send an email to ABDConnect Support at <a href="mailto:abdconnectsupport@theabdteam.com">abdconnectsupport@theabdteam.com</a>. You will receive instructions on how to access <a href="https://clientportal.vertafore.com/Login/1006527-1">ABDConnect</a> on your iPhone, Android or Windows device.
<br>
<br>
<strong>Support for Clients</strong>
<br>
<br>
We have designed the ABD COVID-19 Communications portal to share with you timely and helpful insights as you manage through this emerging pandemic. Our teams will be regularly sharing information pertaining to risk management, benefits, human resources, retirement consulting, and private client and life insurance. Please follow us on <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/company/the-abd-team/">LinkedIn</a> and <a href="https://twitter.com/theabdteam">Twitter</a> for alerts on new posts.
<br>
<br>
<b>ABD Accounts Receivable</b>
<br>
<br>
Given the current environment, including the mandate to shelter in place for at least the next 3 weeks and maybe beyond, ABD is requesting that our clients remit premium via electronic payments (ACH or Wire) so as to insure timely processing. We truly appreciate your cooperation in these uncertain times. Our banking information can be found on ABD Invoices. If you are not the correct contact, please forward to your AP team. If there are any questions or a need for ABD’s routing information, please contact Linda Wang at 650-488-3292 or <a href="mailto:Linda.Wang@theabdteam.com">Linda.Wang@theabdteam.com</a> or <a href="mailto:ap.ar@theabdteam.com">ap.ar@theabdteam.com</a>.
<br>
<br>
While, today, we focus on <strong><em>work</em></strong> and <strong><em>love</em></strong>, we look forward to the future when we can resume <strong><em>play</em></strong>.
<br>
<br>
Thank you for the privilege of serving you.
<br>
<br>
Most sincerely,</div>
