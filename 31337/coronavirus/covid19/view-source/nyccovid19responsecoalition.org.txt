    <h2>Disclaimer</h2>
    <p>This document is a consensus statement that reflects the opinions of a coalition of physicians involved in the care of COVID19 patients, public health professionals, environmental services professionals, experienced community organizers, activists, healers, and journalists. We strive to review and interpret the latest evidence in scientific literature, public policy, and media in order to make our recommendations on best practices in our communities. As the scale and sequelae of the COVID19 pandemic change at an unprecedentedly rapid pace, we present our recommendations with the explicit understanding that they are subject to ongoing change, evolution, and improvement. We welcome the input of other professionals, experienced organizers, and experienced organizations with expertise and guidance in these topics. We extend an invitation to all interested parties to contact our coalition to participate and collaborate for the safety of our communities.</p>
