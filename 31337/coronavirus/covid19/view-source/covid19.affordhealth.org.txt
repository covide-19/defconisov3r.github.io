                    <h3>Screening Criteria</h3>
                    <div class="media-left">

                    </div>

				<div class="media wow fadeInRight">
                    <h3>Take Appropriate Action</h3>
                    <div class="media-left">
                        <a href="https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/if-you-are-sick/steps-when-sick.html?CDC_AA_refVal=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.cdc.gov%2Fcoronavirus%2F2019-ncov%2Fabout%2Fsteps-when-sick.html" target="_blank"><i class="icon-lg ion-android-call"></i></a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="media-body media-middle">
                        <p>(March 12th) Best evidence suggests the following action for COVID-19 testing:</p>
						<p> <b> under age 65 with mild flu symptoms: </b> Do not go directly to the hospital or emergency room. Contact your local medical center about access to a quick testing center available in many states. Medical professionals will provide a management & quarentine plan.</p>
						<p> <b> older than 65 with mild flu symptoms: </b> Do not hesitate to call your hospital system and explain your symptoms. They will advise a plan to admit you to the hospital if appropriate. </p>
						<p> <b> Anyone with severe flu symptoms (high fever, difficulty breathing): </b> Seek immediate medical attention at your local emergency room. </p>
