<p>This site is intended to help local communities support and help each other during this current time of need and in the coming months ahead. It&#8217;s purpose is to provide a platform to reach out for help from the local community or as a network for those wishing to help others. You can register your status as one of two categories and you can change this status after youâ€™ve registered should your situation change.</p>



<h3>Why register if you are self-isolated</h3>



<p>If you are self-isolated other users in your community will see and recognise that you may be in need of assistance.</p>



<h3>Why register if you are NOT self-isolated</h3>



<p>If you are not self-isolating then you can help others. This may involve a number of activities such as nursing, caring, shopping, deliveries, house repairs or other essential things that might otherwise be a burden for the people involved or other support services.<br></p>
