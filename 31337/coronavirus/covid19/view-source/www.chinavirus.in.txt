<div class='post-header'>

<h2 style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #3c4245; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 22px; font-stretch: normal; line-height: 22px;">

<div>
<ul style="box-sizing: border-box; color: #3c4245; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; padding-left: 20px; width: 382.19px;">
<li style="box-sizing: border-box;">If you are healthy, you only need to wear a mask if you are taking care of a person with suspected 2019-nCoV infection.</li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box;">Wear a mask if you are coughing or sneezing.</li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box;">Masks are effective only when used in combination with frequent hand-cleaning with alcohol-based hand rub or soap and water.</li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box;">If you wear a mask, then you must know how to use it and dispose of it properly.</li>
</ul>
