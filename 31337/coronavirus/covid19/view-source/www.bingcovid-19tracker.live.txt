<a href='https://www.bingcovid-19tracker.live/2020/03/bing-covid-19-tracker-coronavirus.html'>Bing COVID-19 Tracker | Real time Coronavirus tracker [Fully Updated]</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2899384201225702793' itemprop='articleBody'>
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
<div style="text-align: left;">
<h2 style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: x-large;">Bing Covid-19 Tracker | Latest Coronavirus Updates</span></h2>
<span style="font-size: small;">If you want to know the live updates of people who got infected by the coronavirus, You can get this information through <b>Bing Covid-19 Tracker or Coronavirus Tracker</b> launched by the Microsoft.</span><br />
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: small;">Covid-19 is spreading ruthlessly across the world. Thousands of people are getting infected by this on a daily basis across the countries.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: small;">Bing Covid-19 Tracker is the <b><i>trusted</i> interactive map tool</b> which can provide the number of people who got infected and the people who died by the coronavirus because it has been launched by Microsoft which is a world's leading IT company. &nbsp;</span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kLjvMfcPW74/XnN9BfncxVI/AAAAAAAABio/nbS0wW3PYhM4i0vH6qltPgeP-eoUbmVlgCLcBGAsYHQ/s1600/Bing%2BCovid-19%2BTracker.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="1600" data-original-width="640" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kLjvMfcPW74/XnN9BfncxVI/AAAAAAAABio/nbS0wW3PYhM4i0vH6qltPgeP-eoUbmVlgCLcBGAsYHQ/s1600/Bing%2BCovid-19%2BTracker.png" /></a></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<span style="font-size: small;">You should not worry about the trustworthiness about the statistics because Michael Schechter, General Manager for Bing Growth and Distribution at Microsoft said, "Lots of Bing folks worked this past week to create a mapping and authoritative news resources for Covid-19 info."</span></div>
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;">The Bing Coronavirus Tracker has taken the statistics from the authoritative organisations like the World Health Organization (WHO), the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control (ECDC) and Wikipedia. </span><br />
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;">Bing Covid-19 tracker displays the coronavirus cases into three categories: Active, Recovered and Fatal. It is a very user-friendly tracker and you can filter it down to any geographical area whose Coronavirus data you want.</span><br />
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;">This Coronavirus tracker is responsive so that you can see the data on any device like desktop, smartphone, tablet etc.</span><br />
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span><span style="font-size: small;">
</span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-size: medium;">If you want the Coronavirus cases data for the USA, Bing Covid-19 Tracker will provide it for each and every state of the USA. As far as other countries are concerned, the data of the states of the different countries are not prov</span>ided. </span><br />
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: small;"><br /></span>
<br />
Covid-19 is a member of Coronavirus which is a family of a certain type of viruses. Coronavirus group contains SARS-CoV, MERS-CoV etc. SARS-CoV was viral in 2002-03 and MERS-CoV was viral in 2012-13 in the middle-east. MERS-CoV was originated from a Camel. The origination source of SARS-CoV was a Bat. Covid-19 is also known as 2019-nCoV(novel coronavirus). This virus was discovered on 31st December 2019 in Wuhan(China). The source of this virus is believed to be a bat.&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
Symptoms: Cough(Dry), Runny Nose, Fever, headache, chills, sore throat, body pain. It is very hard to distinguish the coronavirus from the normal flu on the basis of sole symptoms, therefore laboratory tests are done to find the presence of Coronavirus in the human body.&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
The incubation period of the Covid-19 is 2-11 days. It means if you are infected with Covid-19, it will take 2-11 days to show the symptoms.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
It has been observed in the studies that the more contagious the disease is, the lower will be its mortality rate. For example, the mortality rate of Ebola virus was 70%, but only 3,400 cases of Ebola virus were found. The common flu is very contagious, but its mortality rate is 0.01%. The mortality rate of SARS-CoV was 10%.</div>
