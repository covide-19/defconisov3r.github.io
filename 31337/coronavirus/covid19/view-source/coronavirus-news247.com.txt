<h3>How to protect CORONAVIRUS</h3>



<strong>Wash your hands frequently</strong>



<p>Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water.</p>



<p><strong>Why?</strong>&nbsp;Washing your hands with soap and water or using alcohol-based hand rub kills viruses that may be on your hands.</p>



<p></p>



<div class="wp-block-button"><a class="wp-block-button__link no-border-radius" href="http://coronavirus-news247.com/how-to-protect-coronavirus/">Learn More</a></div>
</div>



<div class="wp-block-column">
<h3>What is a coronavirus</h3>



<p>Coronaviruses (CoV) are a large family of viruses that cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as&nbsp;Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS-CoV)&nbsp;and&nbsp;Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV).&nbsp;A novel coronavirus (nCoV)&nbsp;is a new strain that has not been previously identified in humans.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>



<div class="wp-block-button"><a class="wp-block-button__link no-border-radius" href="http://coronavirus-news247.com/what-is-a-coronavirus/">Learn More</a></div>
</div>



<div class="wp-block-column">
<h3>Symptoms of coronavirus</h3>



<p>The symptoms of coronavirus are:</p>



<ul><li>a cough</li><li>a high temperature</li><li>shortness of breath</li></ul>



<p>But these symptoms do not necessarily mean you have the illness.</p>



<p>The symptoms are similar to other illnesses that are much more common, such as cold and flu.</p>



<p></p>



<div class="wp-block-button"><a class="wp-block-button__link no-border-radius" href="http://coronavirus-news247.com/common-questions-coronavirus/">Learn more</a></div>
</div>
</div>



<h2>Get Ready for Coronavirus Now</h2>



<p class="has-text-color has-background has-very-light-gray-color has-very-dark-gray-background-color">
Have supplies on hand<br>Contact your healthcare provider to ask about obtaining extra necessary medications to have on hand in case there is an outbreak of COVID-19 in your community and you need to stay home for a prolonged period of time.<br>
 If you cannot get extra medications, consider using mail-order for medications.<br>
 Be sure you have over-the-counter medicines and medical supplies (tissues, etc.) to treat fever and other symptoms. <br>
Most people will be able to recover from COVID-19 at home.<br>
 Have enough household items and groceries on hand so that you will be prepared to stay at home for a period of time.
</p>
