<p class="article-heading-des" style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;"><span style="color: #A9A9A9;-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;"><span style="font-size: 24px;-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;"><em style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Countries around the world are stepping up efforts to tackle a new coronavirus that originated in China's Wuhan city.</em></span></span></p>

<p class="speakable" style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">The World Health Organization (WHO) has declared a global health emergency over a new coronavirus that has killed more than 4,600 people worldwide following an outbreak in the central Chinese city of Wuhan<span style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;">.</span></p>

<p class="speakable" style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">More than 126,000 cases of the infection have been reported globally, most of them in mainland China.</p>

<p class="speakable" style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Here is what you need to know:</p>

<h2 style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;display: block;margin: 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 34px;font-style: normal;font-weight: bold;line-height: 150%;letter-spacing: normal;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">What is coronavirus?</h2>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">According to the WHO, coronaviruses are a family of viruses that cause illnesses ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) and the Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS).</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">These viruses were originally transmitted between animals and people. SARS, for instance, was transmitted from civet cats to humans while MERS moved to humans from a type of camel.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Several known coronaviruses are circulating in animals that have not yet infected humans.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">The name coronavirus comes from the Latin word corona, meaning crown or halo. Under an electron microscope, the image of the virus looks like a solar corona.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">The novel coronavirus, identified by Chinese authorities on January 7 and since named COVID-19, is a new strain that had not been previously identified in humans. Little is known about it, although human-to-human transmission has been confirmed.</p>

<h2 style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;display: block;margin: 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 34px;font-style: normal;font-weight: bold;line-height: 150%;letter-spacing: normal;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">What are the symptoms?</h2>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">According to the WHO, signs of infection include fever, cough, shortness of breath and breathing difficulties.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">In more severe cases, it can lead to pneumonia, multiple organ failure and even death.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;"><span style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Current estimates of the incubation period - the amount of time between infection and the onset of symptoms - range from one to 14 days. Most infected people show symptoms within five to six days.</span></p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;"><span style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;">However, infected patients can also be asymptomatic, meaning they do not display any symptoms despite having the virus in their systems.</span></p>

<p style="text-align: left;-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Read more on what the coronavirus does to your body if you catch it below- <img data-file-id="5058479" src="../mcusercontent.com/73b8d043b08c11b68578aef66/images/f9ce8de3-b13f-4511-967d-5e5a5bda8263.jpg" style="border-image: none 100% / 1 / 0 stretch;width: 786px;height: 724px;margin: 0px;float: left;-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;border: 0;outline: none;text-decoration: none;max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;" width="786" height="724"><br style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;">

                <h2 style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;display: block;margin: 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 34px;font-style: normal;font-weight: bold;line-height: 150%;letter-spacing: normal;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">How deadly is it?</h2>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">With more than 4,200 recorded deaths, the number of fatalities from this new coronavirus has surpassed the toll of the 2002-2003 SARS outbreak, which also originated in China.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;"><span style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;box-sizing: border-box !important;">SARS killed about 9 percent of those it infected - nearly 800 people worldwide and more than 300 in China alone.&nbsp;</span>MERS, which did not spread as widely, was more deadly, killing one-third of those it infected.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">While the new coronavirus is more widespread in China than SARS in terms of case numbers, the mortality rate remains considerably lower at approximately 2 percent, according to the WHO.</p>

<h2 style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;display: block;margin: 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 34px;font-style: normal;font-weight: bold;line-height: 150%;letter-spacing: normal;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Where have cases been reported?</h2>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Most cases and deaths have been reported in China - the vast majority in Hubei province.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Deaths have also been confirmed in Hong Kong, the Philippines, Japan, France, Taiwan, South Korea, Italy and Iran.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">The virus has spread to many countries in the Asia-Pacific region as well as in Europe, North America, the Middle East and Africa.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">The majority of cases outside China are among people who recently travelled to the country, however, instances of human-to-human transmission have been recorded in several countries and questions have been raised about cases with no apparent link to China.</p>

<h2 style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;display: block;margin: 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 34px;font-style: normal;font-weight: bold;line-height: 150%;letter-spacing: normal;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">What is being done to stop it from spreading?</h2>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Scientists around the globe are racing to develop a vaccine but have warned that one is unlikely to be available for mass distribution before 2021.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Meanwhile, Chinese authorities have effectively sealed off Wuhan&nbsp;and placed restrictions on travel to and from several other cities, affecting some 60 million people.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Many international airlines have cancelled flights to China. Some countries have banned Chinese nationals from entering their territories and several more have evacuated their citizens from Wuhan.</p>

<h2 style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;display: block;margin: 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 34px;font-style: normal;font-weight: bold;line-height: 150%;letter-spacing: normal;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Where did the virus originate?</h2>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">Chinese health authorities are still trying to determine the origin of the virus, which they say likely came from a seafood market in Wuhan where wildlife was also traded illegally.</p>

<p style="-webkit-box-sizing: border-box;-moz-box-sizing: border-box;margin: 10px 0;padding: 0;color: #212832;font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, Verdana, sans-serif;font-size: 16px;line-height: 150%;text-align: left;box-sizing: border-box !important;">On February 7, Chinese researchers said the virus could have spread from an infected animal species to humans through illegally-trafficked pangolins, which are prized in Asia for food and medicine.</p>
