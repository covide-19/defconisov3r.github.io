<h3>Overview of Current Guidance</h3>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Classes </strong>
	<ul>
		<li>All classes are cancelled starting Friday, March 13, 2020.</li>
		<li>Online instruction will begin for ALL classes on Monday, March 30, and continue for the remainder of the semester. </li>
	</ul>
	</li>
	<li><strong>Undergraduate housing </strong>
	<ul>
		<li>Undergraduates should not return to campus after spring break.</li>
		<li>We are requiring undergraduates to depart from campus residences no later than noon on Tuesday, March 17.</li>
	</ul>
	</li>
	<li><strong>International travel</strong>:&nbsp;MIT-sponsored international travel is suspended for all faculty, students, postdocs, and staff.</li>
	<li><strong>Domestic travel</strong>: We strongly encourage people to postpone all non-essential domestic travel. All travel by those remaining on campus must be registered in the confidential <a href="http://kb.mit.edu/confluence/display/istcontrib/MIT+Travel+Registry" target="_blank">MIT travel registry</a>.</li>
	<li><strong>Personal travel</strong>: We strongly encourage people to postpone all non-essential personal travel. We ask those on campus to please register personal travel in the confidential  <a href="http://kb.mit.edu/confluence/display/istcontrib/MIT+Travel+Registry" target="_blank" title="http://kb.mit.edu/confluence/display/istcontrib/MIT+Travel+Registry">MIT travel registry</a>.</li>
	<li><strong>Events and meetings</strong>
		<ul>
		<li>All events of 25 people or more must be postponed, cancelled, or moved online.</li>
		<li>As of Friday, March 13, 2020, all undergraduate student group activities and residence hall events, regardless of size, are cancelled.</li>
		</ul>
	</li>
	<li><strong>Staff</strong>
		<ul>
			<li>Starting March 13, 2020, and until we communicate otherwise, all MIT employees who can work from home should make arrangements with their supervisors to do so. </li>
			<li>For these employees whose work cannot be done remotely, we encourage implementation of&nbsp;<a aria-describedby="slack-kit-tooltip" delay="150" href="https://medical.mit.edu/three-ways-to-protect" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">social distancing and other best practices</a>, and development of individualized workplans that minimize&nbsp;<a aria-describedby="slack-kit-tooltip" delay="150" href="https://medical.mit.edu/faqs/COVID-19#faq-12" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">close contact</a>.</li>
		</ul>
	</li>
	<li><strong>Visitors</strong>:&nbsp;Visitors from countries that the CDC finds&nbsp;<a href="https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/travelers/index.html" target="_blank" title="https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/travelers/index.html">have “widespread sustained (ongoing) transmission” of Covid-19</a>&nbsp;cannot join us on campus until they have successfully completed 14 days of self-quarantine.</li>
</ul>
