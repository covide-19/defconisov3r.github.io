<h1>COVID-19 Screening Tool</h1>



<p>You have reached the Manitoba COVID-19 Screening Tool.</p>



<p>In order to determine if you should contact <strong>Health Links – Info Santé</strong> (<a href="tel:12047888200">204-788-8200</a> or toll-free at <a href="tel:18883159257">1-888-315-9257</a>) or seek other medical advice, you will be asked to respond to a few questions below.</p>



<p><strong>Please note:</strong></p>



<p>Public health officials are strongly advising all Manitobans, including health-care providers, to cancel or postpone any non-essential international travel. In addition, <strong>public health officials are recommending all international travellers self-isolate and self-monitor for symptoms for 14 days after returning to Canada</strong>.</p>



<p>Public health officials also strongly urge anyone who has cold or flu-like symptoms, such as a cough, fever, runny nose, sore throat, weakness or headache, to self-isolate for 14 days.</p>



<p>Social distancing is used to intentionally reduce close contact between people to try to stop the progression of community transmission of any virus. Social distancing strategies for all Manitobans include: </p>



<div class="wp-block-advgb-list important-list"><ul class="advgblist-098e79a1-1ce7-4f02-b7cb-fc607f1a1638 advgb-list advgb-list-warning"><li>Cancelling or postponing any large-scale events with more than 250 attendees; </li><li>Minimizing prolonged (more than 10 minutes), close (less than two metres) contact between other individuals in public; </li><li>Avoiding greetings that involve touching such as handshakes; </li><li>Disinfecting frequently used surfaces; </li><li>Following public health advice related to self-monitoring and self-isolation if you have travelled or have been exposed to someone ill with the virus; and </li><li>Considering avoiding travel, crowded places and events, especially if you are at higher risk.</li></ul></div>



<p>We remind you to protect yourself while out in public. Please <strong>wash your hands frequently</strong>, and maintain a distance of about 2 metres from others.</p>



<p> For the most up-to-date, factual, information on COVID-19 please visit <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://manitoba.ca/covid19/" target="_blank">manitoba.ca/covid19/</a>. </p>
