			<p>This test consists of two types of questions. <strong>The former</strong> are related to your current state and are aimed at identifying the early symptoms of coronavirus (for the most part coincide with the symptoms of influenza and SARS). <strong>The second type</strong> of question is aimed at identifying the potential for COVID-19 infection.</p>
<p>This test is for informational and warning purposes only. Answering questions, you will understand how you can protect yourself to the maximum and reduce the risk of potential infection, as well as in the presence of any symptoms you should consult a doctor.</p>
<p>There will also be 2 questions about the current situation in your country and city, you can find out using the <a href="https://coronavirus-test.info/en-gb/map/">online distribution map</a>.<br />
