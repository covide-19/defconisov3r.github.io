<div class="wp-block-columns">
<div class="wp-block-column" style="flex-basis:66.66%">
<p>Reality has shifted for the Leading Health Systems,
suppliers, and service providers amid the outbreak of COVID-19. Despite the
immense challenges, The Academy has been flooded by offers to share valuable
resources, pertinent experiences, and creative solutions for the benefit of our
broader community. It is in this spirit that we decided to launch our COVID-19
Resource Center. </p>



<p>On this site, you’ll find the best of information on
COVID-19 preparation, response, and recovery from governments, suppliers, and
providers. All resources are carefully vetted, organized by critical topical
domain, and free for use and redistribution. Additionally, you’ll find
information on our member Townhalls—Academy hosted virtual conversations
designed to facilitate real time sharing and problem solving.</p>



<p>We sincerely hope this resource can play a role in helping
you and your teams lead through this unprecedented crisis. We are here to help.
