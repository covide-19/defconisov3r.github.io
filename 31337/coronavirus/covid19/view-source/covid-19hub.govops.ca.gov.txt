        <h1>COVID-19 State of California Government Operational Guidance</h1>
        <div class="lead">This COVID-19 Operational Guidance Hub provides State of California executive leadership with guidance for the COVID-19 response. COVID-19 Operational Guidance is being updated regularly as the response evolves. Department leaders are expected to keep themselves apprised of the State's latest guidance and operationalize this guidance relevant to their Department operations. All guidance will also be sent out via email, but this Hub is intended to make clear the latest and most up to date information on the State's response.</div><hr class="my-4">
